GAMEHACKS

Cross-Platform Web Automation Hub

OPERATIONAL MANUAL

1. INSTALLATION

Open your browser's bookmarks bar (Ctrl+Shift+B). Click and DRAG the colored buttons below directly onto that bar.

2. NAVIGATION

Go to the website listed in the tool's [TARGET SITE]. These scripts only work when you are actually on the right page.

3. EXECUTION

Once on the site, click the bookmark. For "Scrapers", stay on the page until the data exports to a new window.
